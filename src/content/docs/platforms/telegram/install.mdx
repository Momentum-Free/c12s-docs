## 1) Adding the C12s Bot to your Telegram Group
To add the C12s bot to your Telegram group, go over to the [C12s App](https://app.c12s.ai) hover over the connect button and select "Telegram", it will open a new prompt where you can press to open the bot chat on Telegram, from there you can add it to any group you want. Alternatively you can press [here](https://t.me/C12sbot) to open the bot chat on Telegram.

## 2) Making sure the bot has the correct permissions

When adding the bot to your group, by defaul it will have the following permissions activated:

| Name | Description |
| ---- | ----------- |
| `Admin Rights` | Blank |
| `Change Group Info` | Blank |
| `Ban Users` | Blank |
| `Invite users via Link` | Blank |
| `Pin Messages` | Blank |

<Badge text="Note" variant="tip" /> To ensure that the bot functions correctly, please avoid changing the default permissions. 

---
## 3) Linking the Telegram Bot to your C12s Organization
To link the Telegram bot to your C12s organization, go over to the [C12s App](https://app.c12s.ai) hover over the connect button and select "Telegram", it will open a new prompt where you can copy the verification code.

**insert GIF here**

After copying the code, go over to Telegram open the group that you want to link and type `/verify [code]` and press send, the bot should appear as connected in the C12s App.
